ShieldView

Vulnerability management with KEV tracking, executive scores, and team views

What it does: Ingests Tenable scan data, cross-references CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities, and surfaces findings by team and severity. Tracks remediation status and computes an executive compliance score with letter grading.

Vulnerability Mgmt
🛠️

RemFlow

Remediation pipeline — groups assets, deploys fixes, tracks coverage

What it does: Groups affected assets by CVE to assess blast radius, deploys patches via SCCM/PDQ-style workflows, and tracks deployment progress. Receives findings from ShieldView, applies fixes, then hands off to TheValidator for verification.

Remediation
⚡

TheValidator

Endpoint health checks, GPO drift detection, fix verification

What it does: Performs simulated agent health checks across security tools (endpoint protection, ZTNA, encryption, patching). Detects GPO configuration drift by comparing actual endpoint settings against policy baselines. Verifies that RemFlow-deployed remediations actually resolved the issues.

Health + Compliance
📎

AskClippy

AI chat for your infrastructure — ask about assets, CVEs, health

What it does: Drop a Tenable scan CSV/JSON or point it at your knowledge snapshot, then ask questions in plain English. "Which assets have critical KEV CVEs?", "What's our GPO compliance rate?", or "Executive summary of our security posture." Offline-capable with an animated Clippy assistant.
